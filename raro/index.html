Staking capital in Raro gives you Raro Pool Tokens which earn $RARO, the Raro Governance Token. The distribution of $RARO follows the same distribution as Rari's $RGT, with the difference that the Raro team does not get any allocation.

That's right, we don't get any preallocation of tokens. Instead, we get a percentage of whatever tokens you decide to claim before the token distribution is completed.

Token Distribution

The token distribution plan is the same as Rari's.

Full details of the token distribution curve can be found on Rari's website.

Details of when the token distribution will begin will be announced shortly. We will give at least a week's notice before the token distribution begins.

Fees

$RARO Claim Fee.
There is a scaling fee of 33% to 0% in claiming tokens from the initial token distribution.

Withdrawal fee.
There is a 0.5% withdrawal fee on all withdrawals made from Raro Capital pools. This fee goes towards buybacks of $RoGT and dividend distributions, incentivizing token holders to stay invested and farmers to stay in the pool.
